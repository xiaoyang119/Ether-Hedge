📖 How Delta-Neutral Hedging Works
1
Hold Spot Position
You hold ETH as a spot position. If ETH drops, your portfolio loses value.
2
Open Short Perpetual
Deposit HDGT collateral and open a short perpetual position equal to your spot exposure.
3
Delta Neutrality
With 100% hedge, spot losses = short gains. Net exposure becomes zero.
4
Manage Funding Costs
Perpetual swaps have 8-hourly funding payments — the ongoing cost of hedging.
⚖️ Trade-offs
✓ Benefits
• Eliminates directional price risk
• Adjustable hedge ratio
• Leverage reduces capital
• On-chain & permissionless
✗ Costs
• Funding rate payments
• Full hedge = no upside
• Liquidation risk
• Gas costs
